OVERVIEW CHANGELOG
Brilliant Quotes app is a collection of over 3300 carefully hand picked quotes from more than 230 people. It contains saying from philosophers, writers, spiritual thinkers and other great minds whose brilliant words inspired many human beings. See it for yourself, download it now and get inspired every day!

A new version of the app brings lots of improvements. Cleaner user interface and simpler navigation make the browsing even more enjoyable.

What is more:
  • See “Today’s staff pick” and “Quote of The Day” from the app’s dashboard
  • Browse quotes by author and category.
  • Get daily notifications when Quote of The Day changes.
  • Read short description about an author inside the app and follow a link to Wikipedia page for more information.
  • Access “World’s Top 50” quotes. See what quotes other people liked today, last week and in all times.
  • Choose which authors, categories and quote you want to see using Filters.
  • Use simple search feature to find a quote or author you are looking for.
  • Add an app-widget to your home screen and select which quotes you want to see.
  • And many, many more...

Order processing

We use PayPal and official app stores to process orders. All data are encrypted, order processing is secure. We do not have access to your credit card information.

What we do with the information

We keep your email and order id if you bought the app directly from our website. We use these data only to provide the necessary updates and generate new activation codes for your new phones upon your request.

We do not keep the information about Apple, Amazon or Google store purchases, this information is recorded by the corresponding app store.

We track your support requests to better answer your questions.

We never sell, trade, rent or lease your personal information.

Our apps

Our apps never track your location, network usage or any other activities.

Privacy Policy – CustomCalc (Visacsoft)


Introduction

This is the privacy policy for CustomCalc (Visacsoft), our product including CustomCalc for Android. Specifically, this policy exists to inform you about the types of information we may receive, how we use that information, the measures we take to safeguard the information we receive, the choices you may make and certain legal rights that you have.

Overview

At CustomCalc, we may receive different types of information from our users, such as:

  • Crash and usage statistics from our Products, to help us debug and improve our Products.
  • Personal contact information contained in correspondence via our web site and communications channels. This enables us to offer user support, beta testing communities, surveys, competitions and similar services. When you register for our services we will also collect your email address. We may use this to tell you about our products and services from time to time, unless you indicate during registration, or by clicking an “opt out” link in any message that we send you, that you do not wish us to do so.

In order to help protect your privacy and security, we follow these safeguards:

  • We avoid collecting or storing data entered into password fields while using our Products.
  • We avoid collecting or storing logs of key presses entered while using our Products (no such logs exist).

Measurement data

Our Products collect certain statistics about usage and send these back to Visacsoft. These statistics are used to help us improve our Products.

Sharing Information

We will not share your information, of any kind, neither cookies, log files, statistics, or device identifiers, with any third-party organizations.

Change of control

If we sell or otherwise transfer part or the whole of CustomCalc or our assets to another organization (e.g., in the course of a transaction like a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, dissolution or liquidation), information from or about our users may be among the items sold or transferred.

Policy changes

From time to time we may make changes to this privacy policy, which will be posted on our web sites and relevant communications channels. You are advised to check our web sites regularly to view our most recent privacy policy.

Privacy Policy

Introduction

FatAttitude takes your privacy very seriously.

Any personal information you give us is held with care and security.

Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated. This policy may change from time to time.

How We Obtain Information

Through registration forms, online surveys and through your use of our mobile apps we may obtain information from you.

We will endeavour to make it clear when we are obtaining information from you.

What information we collect

The information that we collect about you depends upon your interaction with our website, apps and services, and may consist of all or any of the following:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your physical (real-world) address
  • Your GPS location (not stored - see below)

Location (GPS) information

We sometimes need to use your physical (GPS) location, e.g. when you are using one of our mobile apps.

For example, if you are using our 'Bus Checker' app, we may need to send your location to our servers so that we can display a list of nearby bus stops to you.

Information about your location is never stored by us and we do not share it with any third parties.

Cookies / Website

When you log on to the web site, our computers may automatically recognise you by use of a "cookie". Cookies are files stored on your computer's hard drive, which ensure that only you can access your own personal information. We do not use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer unless you knowingly and willingly provide such information.

For each visitor to our web site, our web server automatically recognises the following:

  • Your IP address
  • Where you came from
  • Your browser.
  • Any Platform Information (e.g. Windows/ OS X)

How We Use Information

We will never share your personal information with any other party without your permission except for the sole purpose of data verification and supplementation.

The personal information you provide is used both for verification purposes and to ensure that the information and content you receive is both relevant and accurate.

From time to time, we may use your email address to contact you with updates or offers that we believe are interesting and relevant to you. We will ensure that you are able to opt-out of receiving these emails, both at the time of registration (if possible) and also by clicking an 'opt out' link within the emails themselves.

We may research and report on our users' demographics, and activities based on the information we possess. In doing this, we will never identify you individually. We may share these general statistics with advertisers or partners.

We may need to send your personal information to other countries for storage purposes on servers that we rent or own. This includes countries outside the EEC. Your data will not be available to third parties in these locations.

How We Share Information

Your personal information will never be released to any third party without your express permission except in special circumstances such as when the law requires it or other circumstances as detailed below.

We may share our research and reports with advertisers or partners.

Occasionally, we may use third parties to verify or supplement data. For example, we may check that you are living at the address given by verifying this information against information available for that purpose from other sources (e.g. electoral rolls). We may also target offers to you by comparing your information with other statistics to determine whether an offer is likely to be relevant to you. When third parties are employed for these purposes, the confidentiality of your personal information is maintained in strict conformity with this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. Such third parties will agree to maintain the confidentiality and protection of your personal information, at all times during and after providing services.

Accessing and Updating Information.

At any time, you may view and/or change certain parts of the information that we hold pertaining to you, e.g. your name and address. You may view all information we hold about you by contacting us and requesting this. A fee may be applicable. You may also choose not to receive emails from us by opting out using the link in the email itself, or alternatively sending a request to general enquiries at fatattitude dot com.

You can choose whether to accept 'cookies' on your computer. If you choose to reject all cookies you will be unable to use services that require them.

Consent

By giving us your personal information when you register for a website service, free trial of an app or otherwise, you consent to the collection, storage and processing of your personal information by FatAttitude in the manner set out in this privacy policy.

We welcome questions, comments or concerns about our Privacy Policy.

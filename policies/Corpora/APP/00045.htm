Indoor Mapping & Real-Time Indoor GPS

Map your workplace and enable users to easily locate indoor venues on floor maps from web portal and mobile app.  Real-time navigation and wayfinding are now possible with advanced indoor positioning technology.

Mobile Conference Room Booking

Search, locate, and book any conference room. You can now simply pick the room, see it on the map, and book it directly from the Indoor Finders mobile app.  Filter rooms based on preferred meeting time, room type, size, and amenities.

Conference Room Display Panel

Beautifully-designed room display panel instantly enhances your office styling.  Check-in enforcement and early meeting-ending capabilities maximize the room utilization and prevent no-shows.  Unlimited theming.

People Finder: The Corporate Mobile Directory

Corporate phone book on steroids! Find employee contact information, organization hierarchy, and seating location visually on the map. People finder helps ensure that the current seating records are up-to-date.

Space & Seating Management

Manage various types of spaces (seating, reserved, hot-desking, hoteling, etc.) with ease.  Get real-time global occupancy statistics.  Options to use the quick-n-easy seating web portal and/or the full-blown IntelliOffice CAFM system.

Move Scenarios & Block Planning

Create multiple "what-if" move/block planning scenarios and visually evaluate their impacts.  Free more time by delegating seating blocks to department managers.  Import from move personnel files (xls/csv) for fast data entry.

Hoteling &
Hot-Desking

Reserve flexible seating (hoteling/hot-desking) from mobile app, kiosk, or web portal.  Check desk availability from anywhere.  Reservation is instantly reflected in People Finder so that colleagues can locate you through the app.  

Amenity & Asset Finders

Locate any venue on map.  Find printers, first aid, fire extinguishers, emergency exits, restrooms, and other amenities.  Easily locate assets and critical items such as HVAC units, medical supply lockers, and shut-off values.

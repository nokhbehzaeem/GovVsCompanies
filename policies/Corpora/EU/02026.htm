Personal data protection

The GSA processes all personal data in compliance with the applicable rules on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data by the Union institutions, bodies, offices and agencies and on the free movement of such data (currently Regulation (EU) No 2018/1725).

Although you can browse through most of the GSA Website without giving any information about yourself, in some cases personal information is collected and processed (such as data necessary for the subscription to our newsletter and for contacting us). Relevant privacy statements are available for each processing operation under the respective section of the GSA website. These privacy statements will give you details regarding the controller and data protection officer, the purpose(s) of the processing operation, the categories of personal data concerned, the categories of recipients of such personal data, your rights as a data subject and information on the retention period of your personal data.

Privacy Policy

Sophiris Bio Corp. privacy notice

Sophiris Bio Corp. collects Personal Information about you in order to provide the services you request and to tailor our communications to match your interests.

The information we collect

In order to use our website located at sophirisbio.com (the “Website”) or to contact us, you will need to register for an account and provide certain personally identifiable information to us (“Personal Information”), which may include any of the following:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

We will only use your Personal Information for the following business purposes:

  • Creating and maintaining accounts;
  • Respond to emails we receive from you;
  • Communicating with you;
  • Providing the services you request;
  • Sending surveys to you;
  • To analyze, manage and improve the Website;
  • To notify you of special offers and other services that we believe may be of interest to you or to send you current mailings;
  • To notify you that you have been selected as a winner of a contest or sweepstakes;
  • To help us diagnose problems with our server or other technical issues relating to the performance of the Website; or
  • For any other specific business purpose stated at the time you provide your information.

We may also use certain standard technologies such as “cookies,” web server logs, or other statistics programs (“Usage Information”) to track and record how you use the Website. Usage Information is anonymous and does not personally identify you and helps us measure the success of the Website and its features or pages and to improve your online experience. Your browser may have an option that allows you to accept cookies, reject cookies, or receive notification when a cookie is sent, but you should note that the use of such restrictive browser settings may limit your use of the Website.

How Sophiris Bio Corp. shares your information

We do not rent or sell your Personal Information to any third party, but we may share or disclose it as follows:

  • To third parties retained by Sophiris Bio Corp. (who shall be subject to confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those imposed upon us by this Privacy Notice) to the extent necessary to perform any of the business purposes listed above;
  • In connection with the sale or transfer of all or a portion of our business or assets;
  • To comply with any law, regulation or court order, to cooperate with government investigations, to help prevent fraud or to enforce or protect the rights of Sophiris Bio, Corp. or its subsidiaries;
  • As necessary to complete a request you initiated, such as with third parties that are administering on our behalf a contest or sweepstakes that you have entered at the Website; and
  • Or otherwise with your consent.

In all other cases, Sophiris Bio Corp. will obtain your express consent before sharing your information with third parties.

Your choices about receiving email

You can elect to receive emails from Sophiris Bio Corp. by filling out any form on our site. You can also opt-out at any time. by clicking on the link provided at the bottom of each email communication.

Protecting your information

Your email transmissions and/or other communications containing Personal Information may be unlawfully intercepted or accessed by third parties and/or the Website may be subject to hostile network attacks or administrative errors. We cannot and do not guarantee the security of any information transmitted over the Internet. Once we receive your Personal Information, Sophiris Bio Corp. implements a variety of commercially reasonable administrative, managerial, and technical security measures to help protect your Personal Information.

However, even after we receive your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee that your Personal Information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed as a result of a breach of our commercially reasonable efforts or as a result of any other event beyond our reasonable control. Accordingly, we cannot and do not guarantee that your Personal Information is completely secure and safe from such risks. You must also take steps to protect your Personal Information. We urge you to take every precaution to protect your Personal Information while you are on the Internet.

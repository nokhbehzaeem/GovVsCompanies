Use of ‘cookies’

To make this website work properly, we place cookies on your device.

A cookie is a small text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit the website, commonly used as an anonymous unique identifier.

These are sent to your browser from the website that you visit and are stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Type of cookies

  • Session Cookies: These are cookies to collect information about what you do during a browsing session each time you visit our website. When connecting to a server, the device will be allocated a session ID, which enables the server to identify the device during the session and facilitates website usability. These cookies are temporary and are set to be deleted when leaving our website or ending a session;
  • Persistent Cookies: These are cookies that are stored by your Internet browser for more than a session and are set to expire within a defined time period. Persistent cookies remember and maintain the used settings and information, so one does not have to keep re-entering them whenever visiting our website. They also measure the use and interaction.
  • First-Party Cookies: These are cookies that are store and accessed on your device. They might include cookies related to third-party service providers. As a result, first-party cookies might log user-related information accessible to and processed by third parties.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These are cookies that are stored and accessed on your device by third parties determining the purposes and means of the processing of your information logged by such cookies.

We use "cookies" and other web technologies to collect information and support certain features of the ShopHQ Websites or Applications. Generally, the information we collect using these web technologies does not identify you personally; however, it may be linked to information that does identify you personally, such as the information you provide when you make a purchase or register to use a special feature of the ShopHQ Websites or Applications. If you do not wish to receive cookies, you may set your browser to reject cookies or to alert you when a cookie is placed on your computer. You may also delete our cookies as soon as you leave the ShopHQ Websites or Applications.

Shipping and Returns

Free Shipping On All Orders Over $89

Free standard ground shipping on all orders over $89. Not valid on orders shipping to Alaska or Hawaii. Excludes shipping surcharges for select Home items. Order merchandise subtotal must be $89 or greater after all coupons have been applied. Gift Cards are always shipped free and are not counted towards merchandise subtotal for free shipping discount. Discount automatically applied at checkout. Limited time offer!

Always Free Returns In-Store

If you don’t love what you ordered online, we’ll be happy to accept your return in-store with a valid receipt within 60 days and issue a refund. We accept returns in all stores for all online orders except Vintage Items from LXRandCo and Fine Jewelry from Charles & Colvard. Vintage Chanel Items from What Goes Around Comes Around are FINAL SALE and may not be returned. For all other questions about returns instructions view our full Return Policy.

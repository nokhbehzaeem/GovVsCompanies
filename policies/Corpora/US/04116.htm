Privacy Policy

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) values your privacy and is committed to ensuring a quality experience on our website. We do not collect, store, or share any information except that needed to facilitate the transactions you initiate. Please see the Privacy Policy of the U.S. Department of Agriculture, which governs the FSA website. If you do not wish for us to collect web metrics information, you may choose to opt out of metrics tracking.

If you have any questions or comments about the information presented here, please contact askfsa@usda.gov .

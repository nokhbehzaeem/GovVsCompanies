FMSHRC Privacy Policy

The Federal Mine Safety and Health Review Commission will collect no personal information about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information. The only information the Commission automatically collects is the visitor's Internet domain and Internet Protocol address, the type of browser and operating system used to access the site, the file visited and the time spent in each file, and the time and date of the visit.
